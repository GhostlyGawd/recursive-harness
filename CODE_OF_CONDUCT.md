# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want participation in Recursive Harness to be safe, constructive, and
welcoming. Be respectful, assume good intent while evaluating evidence, give
specific technical feedback, and leave room for people with different levels
of experience and different backgrounds.

Unacceptable conduct includes harassment, discriminatory language, personal
attacks, threats, sexualized attention, deliberate exposure of private
information, sustained disruption, and retaliation against a good-faith report.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository issues, pull requests, discussions, private
security reports, and other spaces where someone is representing this project.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through the contact
path on the [GhostlyGawd GitHub profile](https://github.com/GhostlyGawd). For a
security vulnerability or leaked credential, use [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md)
instead. Include only the information needed to investigate; do not publish
sensitive details.

The maintainer may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or
permanently restrict participation when conduct is harmful. Reports will be
reviewed as promptly and confidentially as practical. No response-time or
confidentiality guarantee can be made by a single-maintainer beta project.
