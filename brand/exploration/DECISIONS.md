Soul-finding: *the recorded GAP between a stated claim and its scored outcome — every form is a visible distance closing on a line of truth, written into a ledger that can only grow and never be edited; improvement is that gap shrinking in plain sight, honesty is the gap being impossible to hide.* Distrusted the literal "harness = leather/buckles" costume; grew from real material (prediction rows, calibration report, append-only JSONL, the three loops, the diff gutter, the write-lock guard). **Verdict: APPROVE** — deep, original, material-grounded.

## Verdict: **keep — Append-Only Strata (B)**
Why: it is simultaneously (1) the most soul-true to the core *"a ledger that only grows and is never edited,"* (2) maximally original / un-nameable, and (3) **the only direction that is natively a documentation design system** — which is the brand's primary downstream job (the harness's README + feature catalog are text-heavy). A (45°-everything) and C (radial instrument) are stunning heroes but impractical to carry across many doc pages. B already integrates the calibration soul-widget and the filled/hollow/pending marks.

Kept ideas to preserve as graphics inside the B system (not a re-synthesis): A's *deviation-from-the-line* calibration figure as a recurring signature graphic; C's *concentric return-rings* considered for the loop/cadence iconography.
